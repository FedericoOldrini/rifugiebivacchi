Discover over 5,600 mountain shelters, bivouacs and alpine huts in Italy - free, ad-free and works offline. Mountain Shelters is the most complete guide for your alpine adventures.

KEY FEATURES

THE MOST COMPREHENSIVE DATABASE IN ITALY
Over 5,600 structures from verified sources: Italian Alpine Club (CAI), OpenStreetMap, Wikidata and Refuges.info. Always up-to-date information on facilities, services and contacts.

INTERACTIVE MAP
View all shelters on a detailed map with smart clustering. Easily navigate through thousands of facilities and find the ones closest to your location.

REAL-TIME WEATHER
Check weather forecasts for any shelter directly in the app. Plan your hikes with up-to-date information on temperature, wind and precipitation.

OFFLINE MAPS
All shelter data is available without an internet connection after the first load. Perfect for hikes in areas without network coverage.

SEARCH AND ADVANCED FILTERS
Find shelters by name, region, altitude, available services, opening period, capacity and access time. Automatic geolocation to find the nearest ones.

DETAILED INFORMATION
For each shelter you'll find:
• Name, location and altitude
• GPS coordinates for navigation
• Bed capacity and opening period
• Direct contacts (phone, email, website)
• Available services (restaurant, showers, WiFi, etc.)
• Access time and difficulty
• Photos of the facility

• Track and share your mountain adventures
